  Finding 1 — Size sensitivity: At small (2²⁰), all 3 abstractions collapse to ~50% PPC. At large they recover to 92–102%. This does not appear in the NVIDIA data (single large size   
  only) and is a novel result worth its own figure.
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  Finding 2 — Cross-platform portability: At large, abstractions are 91–102% PPC on AMD, mirroring the ~98–100% on NVIDIA. This is the paper's portability claim made quantitative.
