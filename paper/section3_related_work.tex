\section{Related Work}
\label{sec:related}

\subsection{Portability Framework Benchmarks}

Deakin et al.~\cite{Deakin2019} evaluate BabelStream across multiple programming
models on CPU and GPU, establishing a methodology for bandwidth benchmarking.
McIntosh-Smith et al.~\cite{McIntoshSmith2021} extend this to OpenMP target offload.
Our work extends this line by measuring \emph{all five layers simultaneously} on all
three current GPU vendor platforms and attributing root causes.

\subsection{ECP Application Studies}

Evans et al.~\cite{Evans2022} survey 62 ECP applications and find that $\sim$30\%
use Kokkos, $\sim$20\% use RAJA, and portability is often achieved at $>15\%$
performance cost. We provide the methodology needed to determine \emph{when}
that cost is acceptable.

\subsection{Julia for HPC}

Godoy et al.~\cite{Godoy2023} benchmark Julia GPU performance and identify
significant variance across platforms. We reproduce the DGEMM gap on NVIDIA and
identify its root cause via PTX inspection (Section~\ref{sec:taxonomy}).
