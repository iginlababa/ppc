High-Productivity Programming Systems for HPC Applications


This SI is about the central tradeoff of modern HPC:

Higher abstraction = higher productivity
Lower abstraction = higher control (performance, power, reliability)

High-productivity programming systems are the attempt to cheat this tradeoff.

Not eliminate it.
Manage it intelligently.


exascale systems 

This Special Issue is about how to design programming systems that let humans think less like hardware and machines think more like systems engineers.

This ties productivity directly to real constraints: scale, faults, power.
